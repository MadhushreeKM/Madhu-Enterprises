Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products and services

What is the minimum order quantity?

We have flexible minimum order quantities. For stone products, minimum is usually 1 ton, and for sand products, minimum is 1 cubic foot. Contact us for specific requirements.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we provide delivery services across Bangalore and surrounding areas. Delivery charges depend on location and quantity. Contact us for delivery quotes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. Payment terms can be discussed based on order size and customer requirements.

How can I get a quote for my project?

You can get a quote by filling out our contact form, calling us at +91 9740161629, or sending a WhatsApp message. We typically respond within 2-4 hours.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive bulk discounts for large orders. The discount percentage depends on the quantity and type of products. Contact us for detailed pricing.

Are your products quality certified?

Yes, all our products meet industry standards and are quality tested. We provide quality certificates and can arrange third-party testing if required.
